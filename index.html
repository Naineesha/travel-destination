    Famous Food

    Tirupati's most famous food is the holy Laddu (sweet), a prasadam from the Venkateswara Temple, but other popular local dishes include tangy Pulihora (tamarind rice) and creamy Dahi Chawal (curd rice), often considered a favorite of Lord Venkateswara, all part of the rich South Indian pilgrimage cuisine.

    Famous Food
